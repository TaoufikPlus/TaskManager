About Us

This is a project of TASK MANAGEMENT SYSTEM for a SMALL COMPANY which deals with multiple projects and tasks.Task Management Systems or (TMS) shape an intersection between task manager and information technology. It merges TMS as a discipline and in particular its basic HR activities and processes with the information technology .Task management is the process of managing tasks through its life cycle. The admin is the person who has control over the every execution of the task given to the employees. Employee performs the task and updates the database accordingly maintaining and evaluation of project activities. It allows the company to set goals and accomplish them. It is very useful to motivate the employees to do better, with reference of his previous progress. Managing multiple individual or team tasks require specialized task management software. Specific software dimensions support common task management activities.Our project will help different types of organization in keeping records of the employees.It not only helps the administrator but also the employees as they will get all the update through the software
